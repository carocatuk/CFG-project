The Doggie Den

About us

We are an energized team in the heart of central London with a passion for rescuing mistreated dogs and pups. We offer a range of services to help combat the challenges that lead to the mistreatment of animals, and specifically dogs. Our main focus is our newly developed Education Center where we actually train up young students and volunteers to perform the surgeries and various treatments in our mobile clinics. We arrange for mobile clinics in all Zone 3 boroughs of London. Additionally, the rehabilitation of abused dogs is very close to our hearts and we pour almost a third of our resources into this area. We believe these dogs deserve a second chance and a home where they are treated with love and care. Our team is comprized of highly specialized individuals that are talented in providing love and compassion in places where it does not exist.
